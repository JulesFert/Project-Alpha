The project

This site gathers the Pokémon of the first generation, a captivating celebration of the iconic creatures that have enchanted generations of fans since their debut. The site is an ode to the beginnings of the franchise, highlighting the distinctive characteristics behind each of these fantastic beings. From Bulbasaur to Mewtwo, each Pokémon from this generation is carefully presented, accompanied by details about their lifestyle and characteristics.
